Korean Rice Cake

Description

Tteokbokki is probably THE most famous street food from Korea: chewy rice cakes simmered in an addictive mix of sweet, savory, spicy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pound of rice cake
  • 4 cups of water
  • 1/3 cup of hot pepper paste
  • 1 tablespoon of korean hot pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • gree onions
  • fish cakes(optional)

Steps

  1. Add Water
  2. Add hot pepper paste
  3. Add hot pepper flakes
  4. Add Sugar
  5. add rice cake
